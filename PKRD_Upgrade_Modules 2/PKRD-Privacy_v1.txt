PKRD Privacy Engine (zk-RingCT)

Overview:
- Combines zk-SNARKs and Ring Confidential Transactions (RingCT)
- Hides sender, receiver, and amount
- Uses stealth addresses and one-time keys
- Mimics Monero's privacy with Ethereum-like structure

Features:
- zk-SNARK based proof generation for tx validity
- Ring signatures for sender obfuscation
- Output commitment and decoy inputs
